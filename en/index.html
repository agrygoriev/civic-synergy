Project components

Platforms’ Institutional Development

Support to the statutory activities of the Platforms, enhancing consistency and coherence in their work as well as developing knowledge and skills of their members to ensure that the Platforms’ objectives are effectively met.

Policy Analysis, Advocacy and Stakeholders Dialogue

Support of the analytical and advocacy activities of the Platforms: monitoring and assessing implementation of the Association Agreement and use of the Eastern Partnership opportunities, preparation of analytical papers on various aspects of European integration reforms, organization of discussion events and stakeholder dialogues, conducting advocacy campaigns.

Awareness Raising and Communication

Increase awareness about the Platforms’ activities through development of communication strategies, active interaction with mass media, as well as maintaining web sites and pages in social media. Support for awareness-raising campaign about the goals and opportunities of the Association Agreement, Eastern Partnership initiative, etc.

EU and the Eastern Partnership Outreach

Enhancing interaction between civil societies of Ukraine, the EU and Eastern Partnership region through establishment of an effective information exchange and facilitation of mutual cooperation. Bringing Platforms’ positions and respective analytical products to the attention of key target audiences beyond Ukraine.

Sub-granting support to Platforms’ activities

Provision, on a competitive basis, of the financial support to Platforms’ member organizations to ensure active and coordinated actions in the implementation of the Association Agreement, fulfilment of the Eastern Partnership priorities as well in capacity development of the Platforms.

The project is funded by the European Union and the International Renaissance Foundation and implemented by IRF’s European Program Initiative.
